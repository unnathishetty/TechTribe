Agricultural Activities

Agriculture information refers to data and knowledge related to farming and agricultural practices. This information can include data about crop yields, soil health, weather patterns, and other factors that can impact agricultural production. With the help of modern technology, farmers can collect and analyze this information to make more informed decisions about how to manage their crops and livestock. For example, farmers can use satellite imagery to track crop growth and identify areas that need more water or fertilizer. They can also use weather data to predict when to plant and harvest crops. By using agriculture information, farmers can improve their productivity, reduce waste, and better manage their resources.

Beach & Public places cleaning

Clean beaches have many benefits for human health because the polluted beaches imperil human lives by beach accidents. Many items left on beaches such as broken glasses, sharp metals, or hard plastics may injure beach-goers physically. Also, marine debris such as fishing gear or nets may risk human life on the beaches.

Waste Management

The term solid waste management mainly refers to the complete process of collecting, treating and disposing of solid wastes. In the waste management process, the wastes are collected from different sources and are disposed of. This process includes collection, transportation, treatment, analysis and disposal of waste.

Spreading Awareness among rural people

Public awareness is important to increase enthusiasm and support, stimulate self-mobilisation and action, and to mobilise local knowledge and resources. Raising political awareness is especially important as policy makers and politicians are key actors in the policy process of adaptation.
